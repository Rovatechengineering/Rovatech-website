Our Story

Revolutionizing automated dispensing technology since 2015

RovaTech Solutions was founded with a vision to transform how essential commodities are distributed in Kenya and across Africa. Our journey began with a simple observation: long queues and inconsistent quality at traditional retail outlets for products like cooking oil, milk, and water.

We recognized an opportunity to leverage technology to create efficient, hygienic, and accessible dispensing solutions. Starting with our first water ATM prototype, we've expanded our product line to include oil ATMs, milk ATMs, rice dispensers, and custom IoT solutions tailored to specific business needs.

Today, with installations in multiple counties across Kenya and expanding to neighboring countries, RovaTech continues to innovate and set new standards in automated dispensing technology.

Our commitment to innovation has positioned us as the go-to provider for automated dispensing solutions in East Africa. We continuously invest in research and development to ensure our products meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Beyond our standard product offerings, we specialize in custom IoT solutions that integrate seamlessly with your existing operations, providing real-time data and analytics to help you make informed business decisions.
