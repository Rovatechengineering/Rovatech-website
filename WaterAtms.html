How to Operate a Water Vending ATM Machine

Operating a water vending machine is relatively straightforward, and our water ATMs are designed to be user-friendly.

  1. Connect the machine to a power source and turn it on using the designated power button or switch.
  2. Choose your preferred payment method, which can include cash, coins, credit/debit cards, or mobile payments like M-Pesa.
  3. Select the desired quantity of water using the provided touch screen or buttons. The machine offers options such as liters or custom volumes.
  4. After the payment is successfully processed and the water quantity is selected, position your container beneath the dispensing area.
  5. Wait for the machine to fill your container with purified water.
  6. Depending on the water machine model, you may also have the option to choose hot or cold water.

Note: Specific operating instructions may vary depending on the model and features of the water vending machine. Please consult the machine's user manual or contact RovaTech's customer support for additional guidance or troubleshooting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does a Water Vending Machine work?

A water vending machine purifies tap water through a filtration system and dispenses it into containers such as bottles or jugs. Depending on the model, the water ATM machine may also add minerals or flavors to the water.

Are water vending machines more cost-effective than buying bottled water?

Water vending machines are more cost-effective than buying bottled water in the long run. The cost of bottled water can add up quickly, while the machines offer a more affordable and sustainable option. Additionally, using the vending machine can reduce plastic waste, which is better for the environment. However, the cost-effectiveness of using a water vending machine versus buying bottled water can depend on various factors such as location, availability, and usage.

What are the benefits of using a water vending ATM machine?

Using a water vending ATM machine offers several benefits. You have 24/7 accessibility to purified water, customizable water quantity options, multiple payment methods, high-quality filtration systems, energy efficiency, and the convenience of self-service operation.

Is the water dispensed by the water vending machine safe to drink?

Yes! The water dispensed by water vending ATM machines is safe to drink. These water ATM machines are equipped with advanced filtration systems that remove impurities, contaminants, and bacteria.

How often must the water vending ATM machine be cleaned and maintained?

The frequency of cleaning and maintenance for water vending ATM machines depends on factors such as usage and environmental conditions. However, regular cleaning and maintenance are necessary to ensure optimal performance and water quality. RovaTech recommends following the manufacturer's guidelines for cleaning and maintenance procedures of the Water ATMs.
