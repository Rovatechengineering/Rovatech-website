How you make money with the salad oil ATM vending machine – Maximizing Your Earnings

Salad oil vending machines are becoming a popular investment among entrepreneurs in urban areas. These machines cater to the daily needs of middle-class and low-income earners, providing them with the convenience of purchasing salad oil in desired quantities. Here's a deeper look into how these machines can become a lucrative source of income.

Understanding the Profit Potential

The key to making money with salad oil ATM vending machines lies in the location and the cost-price margin. Ideally, placing your machine in a densely populated area where the demand for salad oil is high can significantly increase your sales volume.

Cost-Price Margin

The profit you make is the difference between the cost at which you purchase the salad oil and the unit price at which you sell it. Our analysis suggests that for every liter of salad oil sold, you can expect to earn a profit of approximately Ksh 35 – 40.

Sales Volume

In an optimal location, it's possible to sell up to 80 liters of salad oil daily. Based on the estimated profit per liter, this can result in a daily profit of around Ksh 2,800.

Monthly Earnings

When you extrapolate the daily profits over a month, you're looking at a substantial sum of Ksh 84,000 in profit, making it an attractive business venture.

Investment Considerations

When investing in a salad oil ATM vending machine, consider the following:

  • Location: Choose a spot with high foot traffic and easy accessibility.
  • Quality of Oil: Ensure you provide high-quality salad oil to retain customers.
  • Machine Maintenance: Regular maintenance is crucial for the smooth operation of your machine.
  • Customer Service: Excellent customer service can lead to repeat business and customer loyalty.
