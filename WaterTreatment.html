RO Water Treatment Process

Our 6-stage filtration process ensures the highest quality drinking water for Kenyan homes and businesses

Stage 1: Sediment Filtration

Removes suspended solids like dirt, rust, and sand particles larger than 5 microns using a polypropylene filter.

Stage 2: Carbon Filtration

Activated carbon filter removes chlorine, organic chemicals, and improves taste and odor of water.

Stage 3: Fine Sediment Filtration

Secondary sediment filter captures particles as small as 1 micron for additional clarity.

Stage 4: Reverse Osmosis Membrane

Semi-permeable membrane removes up to 99% of dissolved solids, bacteria, viruses, and heavy metals.

Stage 5: Post-Carbon Filtration

Polishing carbon filter further improves taste and removes any remaining odors.

Stage 6: Mineral Enhancement

Adds essential minerals like calcium and magnesium back to the water for optimal health benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reverse Osmosis and how does it work?

Reverse Osmosis is a water purification process that uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove ions, molecules, and larger particles from drinking water. It works by applying pressure to overcome osmotic pressure, effectively removing contaminants and producing clean, safe drinking water.

How often do I need to change the filters?

Pre-filters should be changed every 6-12 months, while the RO membrane typically lasts 2-3 years. The exact timing depends on your water quality and usage. Our system includes indicators to alert you when filter changes are needed.

Can the system handle Kenya's hard water conditions?

Yes, our RO systems are specifically designed to handle Kenya's varying water conditions, including hard water areas. The multi-stage filtration process effectively removes minerals that cause hardness while preserving essential minerals for health.

What maintenance is required?

Regular maintenance includes filter changes as recommended, sanitizing the system periodically, and checking for any leaks. We provide comprehensive training and support for all maintenance procedures.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services across Kenya. Our technicians will install the system, test water quality, and ensure everything is working perfectly before handover.
