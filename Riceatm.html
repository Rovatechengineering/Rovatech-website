RovaTech Rice ATM

Our Rice ATM is an innovative automated dispensing system designed to streamline rice distribution for retailers, wholesalers, and agricultural businesses. With precision weighing technology, multiple payment options, and dust-free operation, it ensures efficient and hygienic rice dispensing 24/7.

The machine is designed with both business owners and customers in mind. It reduces operational costs, minimizes waste, and provides a convenient self-service option for customers. With remote monitoring capabilities, you can manage your inventory and track sales from anywhere.

How The Rice ATM Works

Simple three-step process for efficient rice dispensing

1

Select Quantity

Customers use the Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) to select the amount they want to buy.

2

Make Payment

Choose from multiple payment options including M-Pesa, coins, or card payments. The system provides instant payment confirmation and receipts.

3

Receive Rice

The machine dispenses the precise quantity of rice into the customer's container with minimal spillage. The process is completely hygienic and dust-free.
