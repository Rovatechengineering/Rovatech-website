RovaTech PLC Systems

Intelligent control solutions for automated dispensing machines

Our Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) are the brains behind RovaTech's advanced dispensing systems. Designed specifically for the demanding requirements of oil, milk, water, and rice ATMs, these industrial-grade controllers ensure precise operation, reliable performance, and seamless integration with payment systems.

With robust construction and advanced programming capabilities, RovaTech PLCs deliver the precision and reliability needed for 24/7 operation in various environments. Our controllers are engineered to handle the specific challenges of liquid and granular product dispensing with exceptional accuracy.

Our PLC systems feature modular design, allowing for easy expansion and customization based on your specific requirements. Whether you need basic control functions or advanced IoT connectivity for remote monitoring, our solutions scale to meet your business needs.
