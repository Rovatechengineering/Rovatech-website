Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas in Kenya do you serve?

We provide services across all counties in Kenya. Our main service centers are located in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and Eldoret, with mobile service teams covering the entire country.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For emergency repairs, we aim to respond within 4 hours in urban areas and 24 hours in rural locations. Regular maintenance appointments are scheduled based on your preferred timing.

Do you offer service contracts?

Yes, we offer flexible service contracts tailored to your business needs. These can include regular maintenance, priority support, and discounted repair services. Contact us to discuss the best option for your operation.

What materials do you use for circuit etching?

We work with various materials including Copper clad, HCL acid, Hydrogen Peroxide and Polish Remover.

What training do you provide for machine operators?

We offer comprehensive training programs covering machine operation, basic troubleshooting, maintenance procedures, and business management aspects. Training can be conducted on-site or at our facilities.

How does the M-Pesa integration process work?

Our M-Pesa integration service includes helping you set up a business account, configuring the payment system on your machine, testing transactions, and training your staff. We provide ongoing support for any payment-related issues.
