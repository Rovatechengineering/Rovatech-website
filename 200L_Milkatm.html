Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our 200L Milk ATM

What is the power consumption of the 200L Milk ATM?
The RovaTech 200L Milk ATM consumes approximately 2.2 kWh per day thanks to its energy-efficient inverter technology. This is about 40% less than standard milk ATMs, making it ideal for areas with high electricity costs.
How does the remote monitoring system work?
Our remote monitoring system uses 4G/LTE connectivity to send real-time data about sales, inventory levels, and machine performance. You'll receive SMS alerts when milk levels are low or if there are any technical issues requiring attention.
What payment methods does the machine support?
The 200L Milk ATM supports multiple payment options including M-Pesa mobile money, credit/debit cards via an integrated card reader, and cash payments. This versatility helps maximize your customer base and sales potential.
Is training provided for operating the machine?
Yes, we provide comprehensive training for you and your staff on operating the machine, basic troubleshooting, and maintenance. We also offer ongoing support and refresher training as needed.
What is included in the 2-year warranty?
Our comprehensive 2-year warranty covers all parts and labor for any manufacturing defects. It also includes priority technical support and discounted rates on consumables after the warranty period expires.
