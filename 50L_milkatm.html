Frequently Asked Questions

What permits do I need to operate a milk ATM in Kenya?

You'll need a business permit from your county government, a health certificate, and compliance with Kenya Dairy Board regulations. We guide our customers through this process and provide the necessary documentation for approval.

How do I maintain the milk ATM?

Our milk ATMs feature a self-cleaning system that automates most maintenance. Daily cleaning of external surfaces and weekly deep cleaning as per our manual is recommended. We provide comprehensive training and support for all maintenance procedures.

Can the machine operate during power outages?

Yes, we offer solar power compatibility options. The cooling system can be connected to a backup power source or solar system to ensure uninterrupted operation, which is crucial for milk preservation.

What training do you provide?

We provide comprehensive training covering machine operation, milk handling, hygiene practices, basic troubleshooting, and business management tips. Training is conducted during installation and includes manuals and ongoing support.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services across Kenya. Our technicians will install the machine, set up payment systems, and ensure everything is working perfectly before handover.
